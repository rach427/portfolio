    About me

    Hello! My name is Rachael and I am studying full-stack web development through Codecademy. Having held jobs in a variety of fields - ranging from veterinary to insurance to music education - I have decided to pursue a career in coding after it caught my interest not too long ago. Between self-guided study and formal online courses I have gained many new skills, and I aim to keep building on them as I continue to learn! Though I've lived in many different states/countries, I call North Carolina home and I studied music and psychology at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. When I am not coding, you can usually find me playing music, cooking, or crocheting.
